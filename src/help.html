QR Maps

QR Maps helps you get Google Map addresses onto your phone quickly and easily. Check out this video to see how to use it.

  1. Find a location, or generate some directions on Google Maps.
  2. Click the link (it's just above the top-right corner of the map).
  3. Scan the code on your screen using your phone's QR code reader.

Support

This extension is open source, and you can find it on github. If you encounter any bugs, please report them on the issues page.
